Our Story

CoffeeLatte began as a tiny street-side kiosk in 1992, nestled in the heart of a quiet Brooklyn neighborhood. Founded by two college friends with nothing but a shared love for freshly brewed coffee and long conversations, the brand quickly became a local favorite. Word of mouth and the aroma of hand-roasted beans drew in a loyal crowd, and soon the dream expanded into a cozy café where art, culture, and caffeine met. Over the decades, CoffeeLatte transformed from a single location into a digital space where coffee lovers from around the world could connect, share stories, and experience the warmth of a perfectly brewed cup—right from their screens.
